# ideaverse-OS

One-command CLI that bootstraps a position-addressed, harness-agnostic knowledge vault. Karpathy's wiki pattern with conversational ingestion baked in. A 5-phase build interview compiles your purpose, identity, and workflow into lean identity files that Claude Code, Cursor, Aider, Codex, and Gemini CLI all load through their own convention files. Ships with 6 starter templates and 5 bundled skills (capture router, cortex compile/lint/connect, web-clip, yt-light-research).
